Privacy Policy — ShopNicheUS

Last updated: 2026-05-20

What we collect

When you authorize our app on Pinterest, we receive the business account information you explicitly grant access to (account ID, email associated with the account, and performance metrics for ads run on the ShopNicheUS Pinterest Business Account).

How we use it

The information is used solely to manage the ShopNicheUS Pinterest Business Account: creating pins for our own product designs, running Promoted Pin campaigns, reading performance analytics, and adjusting bid amounts based on conversion data.

What we don't do

We do not share, sell, or transfer any data to third parties. We do not access any Pinterest account other than the one that explicitly authorized this app. We do not use the data for advertising, profiling, or research beyond managing our own ad campaigns.

Retention

Performance data is retained for up to 24 months for trend analysis and budget optimization. Authorization tokens are revoked immediately upon request.
